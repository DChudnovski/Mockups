### Phase 1: Manual Selections and Applying Promotions (4 weeks)

#### Features Needed

**47th Street:** 
- **Promotion Center:** Centralized place for creating Promotions, will have a seperate tab for **Make-an-Offer** and that tab will be dormant until *Phase 2*
- Ability to select items from Advanced Search, or from a **Manual Item Picker** in the Promotion Center.
- Properties of Promotions to be pushed to chosen items (Prices should stay the same on the item but it should show the Promotion Percentage and the Promo Price)
- A List of Promos that are both in effect and those that have lapsed

**MSX:**
- Section for viewing currently active promos from SaaS vendors and the ability to see what the relevant items are.
- Ability to see **Vendor Promotion Percent** for an item directly on its stat page.

**NXC:**
- No major changes needed, Did have the question of whether items on Promo should be shown as on sale or just with reduced price with no indication of a  discount (My vote is for showing them as on sale for the psychological effect) (Additionally are we keeping the price )

### Phase 2: Make-An-Offer; Offer Settings; Offer Notifications (6 weeks)

#### Features Needed

**47th Street:**
- Add Make-An-Offer Section to Promotion Center; The Make an offer section will allow you to create "Switches" for a manually selected list of items that allows you to both turn on MAO as well as push the Reserve percent to those items
- Addition of *MAO Enabled* and *MAO Percentage* properties on Items in 47th Street
- Add Make-An-Offer functionality to individual items. Adds a switch that turns on MAO and pushes the corresponding percentage to the relevant items.
- Create the settings section for Offers and what offers get logged
- Offer logs and Spam settings.

**MSX:**
- Offer logs that are searchable and allow for filtering throug multiple params.
- Addition of *MAO Enabled* and *MAO Percentage* properties on items in MSX that can be pushed from 47th Street.

**NXC:**
- Interface for MAO, ability for customers to make offers, whether it be through an AI chat or whatever other interface is decided upon.
- A means of serving MAOs effectivley to the customers who are good candidates for conversions due to it.
